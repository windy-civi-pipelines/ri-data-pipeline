S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
AND WINDOWS
Introduced By: Representative Matthew S. Dawson
Date Introduced: February 26, 2025
Referred To: House Judiciary
(Dept. of Revenue)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 31-23.3-5 of the General Laws in Chapter 31-23.3 entitled
2 "Nontransparent Windshields and Windows" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 31-23.3-5. Penalties.
4 (a) Any person, firm, corporation, or other business entity that installs any sunscreening
5 material which is not in compliance with the provisions of this chapter shall be fined not more than
6 two hundred fifty dollars ($250) for each offense a first violation and shall be fined three hundred
7 seventy-five dollars ($375) for a second violation and five hundred dollars ($500) for a third or
8 subsequent violation.
9 (b) Any person who, firm, corporation, or other business entity that owns or operates a
10 motor vehicle which has installed on it any sunscreening device or material in violation of this
11 chapter shall be fined not more than two hundred fifty dollars ($250) for a first violation and shall
12 be fined three hundred seventy-five dollars ($375) for a second violation and five hundred dollars
13 ($500) for a third or subsequent violation.
14 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC001768
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO MOTOR AND OTHER VEHICLES -- NONTRANSPARENT WINDSHIELD
AND WINDOWS
***
1 This act would provide penalties for second and subsequent violations of installing and
2 possessing a vehicle with sunscreening materials in prohibited by law.
3 This act would take effect upon passage.
