S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
REAPPOINTMENT OF THE MEMBERSHIP OF THE LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO
STUDY THE ENTIRE AREA OF LAND USE, PRESERVATION, DEVELOPMENT,
HOUSING, ENVIRONMENT, AND REGULATION
Introduced By: Representatives Giraldo, Stewart, and Voas
Date Introduced: March 05, 2025
Referred To: House Municipal Government & Housing
1 RESOLVED, That the special legislative commission created by resolution No. 139
2 passed by the House of Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2021, and approved March
3 25, 2021, entitled "HOUSE RESOLUTION CREATING A SPECIAL LEGISLATIVE
4 COMMISSION TO STUDY THE ENTIRE AREA OF LAND USE, PRESERVATION,
5 DEVELOPMENT, HOUSING, ENVIRONMENT, AND REGULATION" and as extended by
6 resolution No. 194 passed by the House of Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2022, and
7 approved March 24, 2022, entitled "HOUSE RESOLUTION EXTENDING THE REPORTING
8 AND EXPIRATION DATES OF THE SPECIAL LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO STUDY
9 THE ENTIRE AREA OF LAND USE, PRESERVATION, DEVELOPMENT, HOUSING,
10 ENVIRONMENT, AND REGULATION" and as extended and amended by resolution 230
11 passed by the House of Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2023, and approved April 6,
12 2023, entitled "HOUSE RESOLUTION EXTENDING THE REPORTING AND EXPIRATION
13 DATES, AND REQUIRING REAPPOINTMENT OF THE MEMBERSHIP OF THE SPECIAL
14 LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO STUDY THE ENTIRE AREA OF LAND USE,
15 PRESERVATION, DEVELOPMENT, HOUSING, ENVIRONMENT, AND REGULATION" is
16 hereby authorized to continue its study and make a report to the House of Representatives on or
17 before June 8, 2026, and said commission shall expire on June 30, 2026; and be it further
18 RESOLVED, That membership in said commission shall be subject to and at the
1 discretion of reappointment by the Speaker of the House through submission of a letter of interest
2 to be submitted to the Speaker of the House by June 1, 2025; and be it further
3 RESOLVED, That the time for reporting authorized by resolution No. 230 and passed by
4 the House of Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2023, and approved April 6, 2023, be
5 and the same is hereby rescinded.

BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
EXTENDING THE REPORTING AND EXPIRATION DATES, AND REQUIRING
REAPPOINTMENT OF THE MEMBERSHIP OF THE LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO
STUDY THE ENTIRE AREA OF LAND USE, PRESERVATION, DEVELOPMENT,
HOUSING, ENVIRONMENT, AND REGULATION
***
1 This resolution would extend the reporting and expiration dates and requires a letter of
2 continued interest to the Speaker for reappointment of membership, to the special legislative
3 commission to study the entire area of land use, preservation, development, housing,
4 environment, and regulation, from June 8, 2023, to June 8, 2026, and said commission would
5 expire on June 30, 2026.
