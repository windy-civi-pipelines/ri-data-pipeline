2025 -- H 5011
========
LC000506
========
S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
2023 AND 2024
Introduced By: Representatives Chippendale, J. Brien, Edwards, Hull, Casimiro,
Paplauskas, Santucci, Newberry, Hopkins, and Fascia
Date Introduced: January 10, 2025
Referred To: House Rules
1 RESOLVED, that the rules of the House of Representatives adopted by the House of
2 Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2023, entitled “House Resolution Adopting Rules of
3 the House of Representatives for the years 2023 and 2024”, be and the same is hereby amended
4 by adding the following new rule thereto:
5 Display of flags.
6 (49) Flags or banners of any size shall not be displayed on any member's desk within the
7 House chamber; provided however, this rule shall not apply to any flag or banner placed on or
8 behind the Speaker's rostrum.
========
