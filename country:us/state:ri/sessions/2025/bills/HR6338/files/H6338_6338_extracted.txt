S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
2025, IN THE STATE OF RHODE ISLAND
Introduced By: Representatives Shekarchi, Blazejewski, Serpa, Chippendale, Fellela,
Costantino, Lima, Noret, Read, and Shallcross Smith
Date Introduced: May 15, 2025
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, National Hospital Day was set aside in honor of Florence Nightingale, the
2 woman who made nursing a respectable profession and who revolutionized the way hospitals
3 were run. The May 12th observance was expanded to a week-long event in 1953, in order that
4 hospitals could use it to plan and implement more extensive public information programs, and is
5 currently sponsored by the American Hospital Association; and
6 WHEREAS, National Hospital Week 2025 celebrates hospitals, health systems, and the
7 women and men who support the health and well-being of their communities through dedication
8 and care from the heart; and
9 WHEREAS, America's Hospitals and healthcare systems deliver 3.6 million babies,
10 employ 7.4 million people, and provide emergency care to 155 million people across the country;
11 and
12 WHEREAS, Hospitals in Rhode Island have generated more than 60,000 jobs and
13 contributed $11.7 billion in total economic output, and $779.3 million in state and local tax
14 revenue resulting in a significant impact on both community health and the economy at large in
15 2024; and
16 WHEREAS, Providing everything from emergency care, labor and delivery, minor
17 surgery and specialized care, hospitals big and small fill a vital need in our communities and
18 every nurse, doctor, practitioner and staff member are essential in the compassionate delivery of
19 these much needed quality services; and
1 WHEREAS, National Hospital Week is a time to raise awareness of the importance of
2 hospitals and health systems and to highlight the critical role hospitals and healthcare
3 professionals have in our local communities; now, therefore be it
4 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
5 recognizes May 11th through May 17th as "National Hospital Week" and acknowledges and
6 thanks Rhode Island's healthcare professionals for working diligently to provide countless men,
7 women, and families with quality health services and for building a brighter tomorrow for future
8 generations; and be it further
9 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
10 transmit a duly certified copy of this resolution to Howard Dulude, Interim President of the
11 Hospital Association of Rhode Island.
