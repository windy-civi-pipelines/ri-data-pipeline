2025 -- S 1104
========
LC002918
========
S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
Introduced By: Senators Felag, and DiPalma
Date Introduced: May 23, 2025
Referred To: Senate Housing & Municipal Government
(by request)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 45-53 of the General Laws entitled "Low and Moderate Income
2 Housing" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 45-53-17. Minimum egress requirements for conventional subdivisions.
4 (a) In the town of Tiverton, any minimum egress requirements established by municipal
5 ordinance or regulation shall apply equally to comprehensive permit applications submitted
6 pursuant to chapter 53 of title 45.
7 (b) The minimum egress requirements applicable to conventional subdivisions shall not be
8 subject to waiver, modification, or reduction for comprehensive permit applications.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC002918
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO TOWNS AND CITIES -- LOW AND MODERATE INCOME HOUSING
***
1 This act would ensure that minimum egress requirements for conventional subdivisions in
2 Tiverton apply equally to comprehensive permit applications and cannot be waived, modified, or
3 reduced for such applications.
4 This act would take effect upon passage.
