S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
LOBBYING REFORM ACT
Introduced By: Representatives J. Lombardi, Hull, and Felix
Date Introduced: January 22, 2025
Referred To: House State Government & Elections
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 42-139.1 of the General Laws entitled "The Rhode Island Lobbying
2 Reform Act" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 42-139.1-14. Prohibited contributions by lobbyists.
4 No lobbyist, governed by this chapter, shall make any political contributions to any
5 member of the general assembly in any year during the period from January 1 through July 1, unless
6 the general assembly has adjourned its business for the year prior to July 1 of the session year.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
