S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
APRIL 1, 2025
Introduced By: Representatives McEntee, Shekarchi, Blazejewski, Chippendale,
Fogarty, Craven, Caldwell, McGaw, Read, and DeSimone
Date Introduced: April 01, 2025
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, The University of Rhode Island is the state’s flagship public research
2 university, recently named the #1 public university in New England by The Wall Street Journal
3 and designated a R1 institution in the latest Carnegie Classification, joining a highly selective
4 group of U.S. universities noted for research excellence; and
5 WHEREAS, URI offers undergraduate, graduate, and professional students distinctive
6 educational opportunities designed to meet the global challenges of today’s world and the rapidly
7 evolving needs of tomorrow; and
8 WHEREAS, URI, as a Carnegie R1 institution, offers education, research, outreach, and
9 economic development programs that contribute significantly to the health and vitality of our
10 state. URI is a key driver of economic and workforce development in Rhode Island. The
11 University maintains strong and robust partnerships with many local, national, and international
12 companies in a variety of industries, including defense, manufacturing, agriculture, aquaculture,
13 and renewable energy. URI also provides research expertise and professional development
14 opportunities to many corporate and community partners; and
15 WHEREAS, With a diverse and vibrant student body representing more than 75 nations,
16 URI offers some 200 bachelor’s, master’s, and doctoral degree programs, as well as study abroad
17 courses, and myriad summer, winter, and honors programs; and
18 WHEREAS, Faculty at the University are distinguished scholars and researchers whose
19 excellence and commitment to their students is unsurpassed; now, therefore be it
1 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
2 celebrates "URI Day" and thanks the University, faculty, and staff for the significant
3 contributions they provide to our state through education, research, and economic development
4 that improves the lives of all Rhode Islanders; and be it further
5 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
6 transmit a duly certified copy of this resolution to Marc B. Parlange, president of the University
7 of Rhode Island.
