2025 -- S 0181
========
LC000148
========
S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
COUNCIL
Introduced By: Senators Sosnowski, Valverde, DiMario, Gu, Britto, Pearson, and
McKenney
Date Introduced: February 07, 2025
Referred To: Senate Environment & Agriculture
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 46-23-4 of the General Laws in Chapter 46-23 entitled "Coastal
2 Resources Management Council" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 46-23-4. Officers of the council — Quorum and vote required for action.
4 The governor shall select from the appointed members a chairperson and vice chairperson.
5 The council shall thereupon select a secretary from among its membership or staff. The council
6 may engage staff, including legal counsel, as it deems necessary. A quorum shall consist of six (6)
7 members of the council. A majority vote of those present shall be required for action, except that a
8 super majority of three-fifths (3/5) of the duly appointed council is required to override a
9 recommendation by the coastal resources management council staff.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
