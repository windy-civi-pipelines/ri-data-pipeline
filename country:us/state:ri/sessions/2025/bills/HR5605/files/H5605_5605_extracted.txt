S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
SPECIAL LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO STUDY AND PROVIDE
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR THE DEVELOPMENT AND IMPLEMENTATION OF AN
ANTI-LITTERING EDUCATION PROGRAM FOR RHODE ISLAND PUBLIC
ELEMENTARY SCHOOL STUDENTS
Introduced By: Representatives Messier, Alzate, Furtado, Kazarian, and Casimiro
Date Introduced: February 26, 2025
Referred To: House Education
1 RESOLVED, That the special legislative commission created by resolution No. 375
2 passed by the House at its January session, A.D. 2023, and approved June 1, 2023, entitled
3 "House Resolution Creating a Special Legislative Commission To Study and Provide
4 recommendations for the Development and Implementation of an Anti-Littering Education
5 Program for Rhode Island Public Elementary School Students" is hereby reinstated and the same
6 members are hereby reappointed, and it is authorized to continue its study and make a report to
7 the House of Representatives on or before February 2, 2026, and said commission shall expire on
8 April 2, 2026; and be it further
9 RESOLVED, That the time for reporting and date of expiration authorized by resolution
10 No. 375 and passed by the House of Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2023, and
11 approved June 1, 2023, be and the same is hereby rescinded.
========
LC002054
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
REINSTATING AND EXTENDING THE LIFE AND EXPIRATION DATES OF THE
SPECIAL LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO STUDY AND PROVIDE
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR THE DEVELOPMENT AND IMPLEMENTATION OF AN
ANTI-LITTERING EDUCATION PROGRAM FOR RHODE ISLAND PUBLIC
ELEMENTARY SCHOOL STUDENTS
***
1 This resolution would reinstate and extend the reporting and expiration dates of the
2 special legislative commission to study and provide recommendations for the development and
3 implementation of an anti-littering education program for Rhode Island public elementary school
4 students from February 2, 2024, to February 2, 2026, and said commission shall expire on April
5 2, 2026.
