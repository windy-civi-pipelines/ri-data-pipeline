LC001084

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
ASSISTANCE PROGRAM
Introduced By: Representatives Nardone, Quattrocchi, Santucci, Paplauskas, Hull,
Cortvriend, Place, Hopkins, Felix, and Shallcross Smith
Date Introduced: February 12, 2025
Referred To: House Finance
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 42-64.25-6 of the General Laws in Chapter 42-64.25 entitled "Small
2 Business Assistance Program" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 42-64.25-6. Micro-loan allocation.
4 (a) Notwithstanding anything to the contrary in this chapter, not less than ten percent (10%)
5 and not more than twenty-five percent (25%) of program funds will be allocated to “micro loans”
6 with a principal amount between two thousand dollars ($2,000) and twenty-five thousand dollars
7 ($25,000). Micro loans will be administered by lending organizations, which will be selected by
8 the commerce corporation on a competitive basis and shall have experience in providing technical
9 and financial assistance to microenterprises.
10 (b) For the FY 25-26, the sum of two million dollars ($2,000,000) is hereby appropriated
11 to this program from the general revenue fund.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
