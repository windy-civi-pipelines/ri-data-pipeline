2025 -- H 5668 SUBSTITUTE A
========
LC001726/SUB A
========
S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
Introduced By: Representative William W. O'Brien
Date Introduced: February 26, 2025
Referred To: House Judiciary
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 4-1 of the General Laws entitled "Cruelty to Animals" is hereby
2 amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 4-1-44. Declawing cats prohibited.
4 (a) For purposes of this section:
5 (1) "Declawing" means an onychectomy or any other surgical procedure to amputate or
6 modify a portion of a cat's paw in order to remove the cat's claws. "Declawing" does not include
7 the trimming of nonviable claw husk or placing nonpermanent nail caps.
8 (2) "Tendonectomy" means a procedure in which the tendons to a cat's limbs, paws, or toes
9 are cut or modified in order that the normal functioning of the claws is impaired.
10 (3) "Therapeutic purpose" means a medically necessary procedure to address an existing
11 or recurring illness, infection, disease, injury, or abnormal condition in the claws, nail bed, or toe
12 bone, that compromises the cat's health. "Therapeutic purpose" shall not include a procedure
13 performed for a cosmetic or aesthetic purpose or to make the cat more convenient to keep or handle.
14 (b) It is unlawful for any person to perform surgical claw removal, declawing, or a
15 tendonectomy on any cat or otherwise alter a cat's toes, claws, or paws to prevent or impair the
16 normal function of the cat's toes, claws, or paws.
17 (c) This section does not apply to a procedure performed solely for a therapeutic purpose.
18 (d) The prosecution of and procedure for discipline for violations of this section shall be in
19 accordance with §§ 5-25-9 and 5-25-15.
1 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect on September 1, 2025.
