LC002316

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
S E N A T E R E S O L U T I O N
SPECIAL LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO STUDY RENEWABLE ENERGY PROGRAMS
Introduced By: Senators Sosnowski, Britto, and DiPalma
Date Introduced: March 07, 2025
Referred To: Placed on the Senate Consent Calendar
1 RESOLVED, That the special legislative commission created by resolution No. 378
2 passed by the Senate at its January session, A.D. 2024, and approved June 13, 2024, entitled
3 "Senate Resolution Creating a Special Legislative Commission to Study Renewable Energy
4 Programs” is hereby authorized to continue its study and make a report to the Senate on or before
5 May 30, 2025, and said commission shall expire on June 30, 2025; and be it further
6 RESOLVED, That the time for reporting authorized by resolution No. 378 passed by the
7 Senate at its January session, A.D. 2024, and approved June 13, 2024, be and the same is hereby
8 rescinded; and be it further
9 RESOLVED, That the resolution is amended by deleting the language “by August 31,
10 2024,” on page 3, line 1.
========
LC002316
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
S E N A T E R E S O L U T I O N
EXTENDING THE REPORTING DATE AND AMENDING THE LANGUAGE OF THE
SPECIAL LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO STUDY RENEWABLE ENERGY PROGRAMS
***
1 This resolution would delete the language “by August 31, 2024,” on page 3, line 1, and
2 extend the reporting date of the special legislative commission to study renewable energy
3 programs from February 14, 2025, to May 30, 2025, and said commission would expire on June
4 30, 2025.
