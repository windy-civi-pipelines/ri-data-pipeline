S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
SEPTEMBER 11 CLASSROOM MOMENT OF SILENCE
Introduced By: Representatives Read, Noret, Paplauskas, Baginski, Fascia, Hopkins, J.
Brien, Chippendale, Casimiro, and Casey
Date Introduced: January 31, 2025
Referred To: House Education
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 16-12 of the General Laws entitled "Rights and Duties of Teachers
2 Generally" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 16-12-3.2. Moment of silence on September 11.
4 On every September 11 in all grades in all public schools, a building administrator or a
5 teacher may allow a brief moment of silence to be observed in memory of the tragic events of
6 September 11, 2001.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========

========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO EDUCATION -- RIGHTS AND DUTIES TEACHERS GENERALLY --
SEPTEMBER 11 CLASSROOM MOMENT OF SILENCE
***
1 This act would allow the school building administration or teachers in all grades to provide
2 the opportunity for students to observe a brief moment of silence on every September 11.
3 This act would take effect upon passage.
