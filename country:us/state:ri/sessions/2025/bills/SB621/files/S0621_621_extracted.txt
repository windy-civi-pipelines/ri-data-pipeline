========
LC001363
========
S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
HOSPITALS -- MENTAL HEALTH LAW
Introduced By: Senators DiMario, Urso, Murray, Lauria, Ujifusa, Valverde, Appollonio,
and Thompson
Date Introduced: March 06, 2025
Referred To: Senate Judiciary
(Dept. of BHDDH)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 40.1-5-41 of the General Laws in Chapter 40.1-5 entitled "Mental
2 Health Law" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 40.1-5-41. Immunity of physicians and surgeons from liability Immunity from
4 liability.
5 Nothing contained herein shall be deemed to contravene the provisions of § 5-37-14, and
6 no physician or surgeon advanced practice registered nurse (APRN) licensed to practice in this
7 state, or having a privilege to practice in this state, and having met the requirements either of the
8 statute or of this chapter, shall be made to answer in any court for his or her participation in any
9 proceeding under this chapter except upon a showing of actual fraud; provided further, that no
10 physician or surgeon shall be made to answer in any court for any damage or injury to any person
11 or thing arising out of a patient’s enjoyment and/or exercise of rights protected by this chapter
12 including, without limitation, discharge, where the enjoyment and/or exercise of the rights, or any
13 of them, are in contravention of either the written orders or prescription or advice of a physician or
14 surgeon or gross, willful or wanton negligence.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC001363
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO BEHAVIORAL HEALTHCARE, DEVELOPMENTAL DISABILITIES AND
HOSPITALS -- MENTAL HEALTH LAW
***
1 This act would provide that advanced practice registered nurses have the same immunity
2 from liability as physicians and surgeons.
3 This act would take effect upon passage.
