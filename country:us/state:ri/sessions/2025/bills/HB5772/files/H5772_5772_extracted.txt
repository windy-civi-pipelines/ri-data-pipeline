A N A C T
Introduced By: Representatives McEntee, Spears, Fogarty, Cotter, Donovan, Azzinaro,
Kennedy, Edwards, Caldwell, and Noret
Date Introduced: February 26, 2025
Referred To: House Finance
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 27-18-69 of the General Laws in Chapter 27-18 entitled "Accident
2 and Sickness Insurance Policies" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 27-18-69. Licensed ambulance service.
4 (a) No individual or group health insurance contract, plan or policy delivered, issued for
5 delivery, or renewed in this state on or after January 1, 2009 shall provide for a co-payment for
6 ground ambulance services in excess of fifty dollars ($50.00).
7 (b) As used in this section, the term “ground ambulance services” shall mean those services
8 provided by an ambulance service licensed to operate in Rhode Island in accordance with § 23-4.1-
9 6. The term excludes air and water ambulance services and ambulance services provided outside
10 of Rhode Island.
11 (c) Subsections (a) and (b) of this This section shall not apply to insurance coverage
12 providing benefits for: (1) hospital confinement indemnity; (2) disability income; (3) accident only;
13 (4) long-term care; (5) Medicare supplement; (6) limited benefit health; (7) specified disease
14 indemnity; (8) sickness or bodily injury or death by accident or both; and (9) other limited benefit
15 policies.
16 (d) Individual and group health insurance contracts, plans, and policies issued for delivery,
17 or renewed in this state on or after January 1, 2026 shall provide reimbursement rates for ambulance
18 services equal to reimbursement rates provided by Medicare for the same medical services. For
19 purposes of this section, "ambulance" means any publicly or privately owned vehicle, designed,
1 constructed, equipped, and operated for emergency medical treatment and/or transportation of
2 persons who are sick or injured.
3 SECTION 2. Section 27-19-60 of the General Laws in Chapter 27-19 entitled "Nonprofit
4 Hospital Service Corporations" is hereby amended to read as follows:
5 27-19-60. Licensed ambulance service.
6 (a) No individual or group health insurance contract, plan, or policy delivered, issued for
7 delivery, or renewed in this state on or after January 1, 2009, shall provide for a copayment for

10 provided by an ambulance service licensed to operate in Rhode Island in accordance with § 23-4.1-

12 of Rhode Island.
13 (c) Subsections (a) and (b) of this This section shall not apply to insurance coverage

15 only; (4) Long-term care; (5) Medicare supplement; (6) Limited benefit health; (7) Specified
16 disease indemnity; (8) Sickness or bodily injury or death by accident, or both; and (9) Other limited
17 benefit policies.
18 (d) Individual and group health insurance contracts, plans, and policies issued for delivery,

20 services equal to reimbursement rates provided by Medicare for the same medical services. For
21 purposes of this section, "ambulance" means any publicly or privately owned vehicle, designed,

23 persons who are sick or injured.
