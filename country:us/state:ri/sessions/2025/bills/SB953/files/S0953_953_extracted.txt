LC001331

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO ELECTIONS -- MAIL BALLOTS
Date Introduced: April 04, 2025
Referred To: Senate Judiciary
(Secretary of State)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 17-20-18 of the General Laws in Chapter 17-20 entitled "Mail
2 Ballots" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 17-20-18. Instructions on ballot.
4 The secretary of state may, if the secretary deems necessary, cause to be printed at the head
5 of the ballot brief instructions for voting and the use of the ballot. The mail Mail ballots shall, in
6 addition to the official endorsement provided by law, bear the words: “Mail voters”, except for
7 those ballots cast in conformance with § 17-20-2.2(g).
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC001331
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO ELECTIONS -- MAIL BALLOTS
***
1 This act would exempt emergency mail ballots from the requirement to have certain
2 wording regarding "mail voters" on the ballot.
3 This act would take effect upon passage.
