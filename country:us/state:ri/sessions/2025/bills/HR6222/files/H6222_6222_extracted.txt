2025 -- H 6222
========
LC002389
========
S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
RHODE ISLAND
Introduced By: Representatives Shekarchi, Blazejewski, Potter, Chippendale, Giraldo,
Edwards, Fellela, Ackerman, Serpa, and Kennedy
Date Introduced: April 10, 2025
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, Each and every day, new people join the national organ transplant waiting
2 list. Sadly, many of them will die because not enough organs are available for transplant, and
3 thousands of others whose lives would greatly benefit from the gift of an organ, eye, or tissue
4 donation continue to wait and hope; and
5 WHEREAS, There are more than 100,000 Americans, with 5,000 in New England who
6 are currently waiting for a life-saving organ transplant; and
7 WHEREAS, Organ donation is oftentimes the only hope at a new life for people with
8 end-stage organ failure. There are over one million life-enhancing tissue transplants each year
9 that are made possible by the generous donation of corneas, bone, skin or other tissue; and
10 WHEREAS, Organ and tissue donor donation can help to save someone's life and
11 benefits up to 50 recipients by simply signing up and enrolling in the Rhode Island Donor
12 Registry when applying for, or renewing a driver's license, or by registering on-line at
13 www.RegisterMe.org; and
14 WHEREAS, In Rhode Island, 99 percent of all people who register to become a donor do
15 so at the Division of Motor Vehicles, and it is fitting that Rhode Island joins in the national goal
16 of signing up 50 percent of the State's driving population to become donors, and is dedicated to
17 making it faster and easier to sign up to save a life through the Division of Motor Vehicles; and
18 WHEREAS, Organ Donation Month pays tribute to organ and tissue donors and their
19 families whose decision to Donate Life enables others to receive life-saving organs and tissues for
1 transplant; now, therefore be it
2 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
3 celebrates April of 2025, as "Organ Donation Month" and urges all the citizens of Rhode Island
4 to take cognizance of this event and participate fittingly in its observance by getting the Heart on
5 their Driver's license, ID or real ID, and register as an organ donor; and be it further
6 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
7 transmit duly certified copies of this resolution to Mr. Walter Craddock, Administrator, RI
8 Division of Motor Vehicles, and Alexandra K. Glazier, President and Chief Executive Officer,
9 New England Donor Services.
