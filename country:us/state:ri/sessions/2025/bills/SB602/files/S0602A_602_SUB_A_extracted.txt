2025 -- S 0602 SUBSTITUTE A
========
LC002169/SUB A
========
S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
ADVISORY COUNCIL
Introduced By: Senator Samuel D. Zurier
Date Introduced: March 06, 2025
Referred To: Senate Commerce
(Commerce)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 42-143-1 of the General Laws in Chapter 42-143 entitled "Science
2 and Technology Advisory Council" as enacted by Chapter 281 of the 2006 Public Laws is hereby
3 repealed:
4 § 42-143-1. Council established. [As enacted by P.L. 2006, ch. 281.]
5 There is hereby created a Rhode Island science and technology advisory council to consist
6 of thirteen (13) members: three (3) of whom shall be appointed by the governor, with two (2) of
7 those so appointed to be designated by the governor as co-chairs; six (6) of whom shall be the
8 members of the science and technology advisory council, as established by executive order 05-07;
9 one of whom shall be appointed by the speaker of the house of representatives; one of whom shall
10 be appointed by the house majority leader; and one of whom shall be appointed by the president of
11 the senate; and one of whom shall be appointed by the senate majority leader. The membership of
12 said council shall receive no compensation for their services. The council shall support the state’s
13 research institutions, promote entrepreneurial development, enable all organizations to become
14 more innovative, and perform any other advisory functions as the legislature may designate.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC002169/SUB A
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO STATE AFFAIRS AND GOVERNMENT -- SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY
ADVISORY COUNCIL
***
1 This act would eliminate one of two (2) conflicting versions of the science and technology
2 advisory council authorizing statute, clarifying the structure of the council.
3 This act would take effect upon passage.
