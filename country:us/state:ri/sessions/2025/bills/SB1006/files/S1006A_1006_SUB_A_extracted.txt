A N A C T
RELATING TO HEALTH AND SAFETY -- VITAL RECORDS
Introduced By: Senator Robert Britto
Date Introduced: May 02, 2025
Referred To: Senate Health & Human Services
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
2 by adding thereto the following section:
3 23-3-10.3. Certificate of birth -- Modification.
4 A person eighteen (18) years of age or older may request that the description of the person's
5 race be modified or amended on their birth certificate in a manner that utilizes culturally sensitive
6 language and terminology. Such a request shall be made through the state office of vital records.
7 The department of health may promulgate rules and regulations and/or develop a process to
8 accommodate all reasonable requests in a manner which uses culturally sensitive language and
9 terminology to identify, modify and/or amend descriptions of race.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
