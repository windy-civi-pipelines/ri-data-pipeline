S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
REVIEW ACT
Introduced By: Representative Joseph J. Solomon
Date Introduced: February 28, 2025
Referred To: House Health & Human Services
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 27-18.9 of the General Laws entitled "Benefit Determination and
2 Utilization Review Act" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 27-18.9-16. Medically necessary treatment -- Justification for denial.
4 Any healthcare service or procedure authorized by a provider to a patient shall create a
5 presumption that the healthcare service or procedure is medically necessary and it shall be the duty
6 of the insurance company to provide justification for any denial of such healthcare service or
7 procedure.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC002314
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO INSURANCE -- BENEFIT DETERMINATION AND UTILIZATION
REVIEW ACT
***
1 This act would require insurance companies to prove that a healthcare service or procedure
2 is not medically necessary.
3 This act would take effect upon passage.
