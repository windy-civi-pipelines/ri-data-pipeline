2025 -- H 5273 SUBSTITUTE A
========
LC000771/SUB A
========
S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
PREVENTION ACT OF 2016
Introduced By: Representatives O'Brien, McEntee, Lima, Fellela, Corvese, McNamara,
Casey, Read, Donovan, and Hopkins
Date Introduced: January 31, 2025
Referred To: House State Government & Elections
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 21-28.9 of the General Laws entitled "The Good Samaritan Overdose
2 Prevention Act of 2016" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 21-28.9-6. Training to administer opioid antagonists.
4 All lifeguards, and park and forest rangers, employed to work at any state or municipal
5 public beach or facility on a full-time basis shall be trained in the administration of opioid
6 antagonists, pursuant to the provisions of this chapter. All state or municipal public beaches and
7 facilities, that employ lifeguards, and park and forest rangers, shall maintain and provide a
8 minimum of four (4) doses of the opioid antagonist.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC000771/SUB A
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO FOOD AND DRUGS -- THE GOOD SAMARITAN OVERDOSE
PREVENTION ACT OF 2016
***
1 This act would require all lifeguards, and park and forest rangers, who work on a full-time
2 basis at any public beach or facility to be trained in administering opioid antagonists. The act would
3 also require all state and municipal public beaches or facilities, that employ lifeguards, and park
4 and forest rangers to keep available a minimum of four doses of opioid antagonist.
5 This act would take effect upon passage.
