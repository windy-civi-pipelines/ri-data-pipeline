LC001052

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
CHARTER OF THE TOWN OF TIVERTON
Introduced By: Representatives Edwards, and McGaw
Date Introduced: March 19, 2025
Referred To: House Municipal Government & Housing
(Tiverton)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. In all respects in which the amendments to the Home Rule Charter of the
2 town of Tiverton, which amendments were adopted and approved by the electors of the town of
3 Tiverton on November 5, 2024, may require ratification, confirmation, validation, or enactment by
4 the general assembly, but in no other respects, the provisions of said amendments to the Home Rule
5 Charter of the town of Tiverton are hereby ratified, confirmed, validated, and enacted. It is the
6 express intention of the general assembly, by the passage of this act, to give effect to, ratify,
7 confirm, validate, and enact those provisions of the said amendments to the Home Rule Charter of
8 the town of Tiverton which require ratification, confirmation, validation, or enactment and, by the
9 passage of this act, the legislature does hereby ratify, confirm, validate, and enact said provisions,
10 but nothing in this act shall be construed to abrogate or impair the powers now or hereafter granted
11 to towns by the home rule amendment and other applicable laws of the State of Rhode Island or
12 those rights retained by said town of Tiverton in its Home Rule Charter.
13 SECTION 2. If any provision of this act, or the application thereof to the town of Tiverton
14 or to any person or circumstances, is deemed invalid for any reason, the remainder of this act, or
15 the application of such provision to said town or other persons or circumstances, shall not be
16 affected thereby and, to this end, the provisions of this act are declared to be severable.
1 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect upon passage.
