S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
2025, IN THE STATE OF RHODE ISLAND
Introduced By: Representatives McNamara, Shekarchi, Blazejewski, Ackerman,
Chippendale, Ajello, Fellela, Shallcross Smith, Tanzi, and Furtado
Date Introduced: April 24, 2025
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, According to the National Infertility Association, 1 in 6 people are impacted
2 by infertility globally; and
3 WHEREAS, The World Health Organization and American Medical Association define
4 infertility as a disease; and
5 WHEREAS, Infertility affects women and men equally and does not discriminate based
6 on race, religion, sexual orientation, marital status or socioeconomic level; and
7 WHEREAS, The LGBTQ community also faces challenges when building their families;
8 and
9 WHEREAS, All people challenged in their family building journey should have access to
10 all family building options; and
11 WHEREAS, Family building options include adoption and medical treatment, such as in
12 vitro fertilization and third-party reproduction; and
13 WHEREAS, Cost and lack of insurance coverage are barriers for many in our State to
14 access the family building option they need; and
15 WHEREAS, Raising awareness of infertility and the barriers faced by the family building
16 community is the first step to removing these barriers; now, therefore be it
17 RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the State of Rhode Island hereby
18 joins RESOLVE: The National Infertility Association, and dedicated volunteers, health care
19 professionals, and members of the infertility and family building community in commemorating
1 and participating in “National Infertility Awareness Week” in the State of Rhode Island; and be it
2 further
3 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
4 transmit a duly certified copy of this resolution to Rhode Island RESOLVE: The National
5 Infertility Association.
