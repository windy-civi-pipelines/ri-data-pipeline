S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
LICENSURE COMPACT
Introduced By: Representatives Hopkins, Paplauskas, Serpa, Bennett, Shanley, Casey,
Donovan, Boylan, Speakman, and Cotter
Date Introduced: February 26, 2025
Referred To: House Health & Human Services
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 5-91 of the General Laws entitled "Interstate Medical Licensure
2 Compact" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 5-91-26. Implementation.
4 This chapter shall be implemented and the state is hereby directed to complete all necessary
5 membership requirements no later than July 1, 2025.
6 SECTION 2. Title 23 of the General Laws entitled "HEALTH AND SAFETY" is hereby
7 amended by adding thereto the following chapter:
8 CHAPTER 104
9 TELEMEDICINE AND OUT-OF-STATE MEDICINE
10 23-104-1. Telemedicine and out-of-state medicine.
11 (a) All in-state residents shall have access to telemedicine in accordance with chapter 81
12 of title 27.
13 (b) Patients may receive telemedicine services, including services from out-of-state
14 providers pursuant to chapter 91 of title 5 ("interstate medical licensure compact").
15 (c) The department of health and the office of health insurance commissioner shall
16 implement rules and regulations, as necessary, to carry out the provisions of this section.
1 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect upon passage.
