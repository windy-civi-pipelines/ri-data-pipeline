S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
PROJECTS
Introduced By: Representative Megan L. Cotter
Date Introduced: February 26, 2025
Referred To: House Finance
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 2-10 of the General Laws entitled "State Forests and Forestry
2 Projects" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 2-10-14. Expansion of department of environmental management personnel.
4 The general assembly shall appropriate, in the fiscal year 2026 budget, a sum sufficient to
5 fund ten (10) additional full-time equivalent ("FTE") positions, to be assigned to the department of
6 environmental management (the "department"). It is the intent of the general assembly that these
7 positions be used in part to focus, support, and expand on the department's efforts in the areas of
8 woodlands, forests, and forestry projects.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.

2 sum sufficient to fund ten additional full time equivalent ("FTE") positions to be assigned to the
3 department of environmental management (the "department"), with the intent to support and
4 expand the department's efforts in the areas of forests and forestry projects.
5 This act would take effect upon passage.
