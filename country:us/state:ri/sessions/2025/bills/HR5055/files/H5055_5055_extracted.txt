S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
J O I N T R E S O L U T I O N
MAKING AN APPROPRIATION OF $500,000 TO AMOS HOUSE
Kazarian
Date Introduced: January 15, 2025
Referred To: House Finance
1 RESOLVED, That there is hereby appropriated to Amos House, a Rhode Island domestic
2 nonprofit corporation, the sum of five-hundred thousand dollars ($500,000), out of any monies in
3 the treasury not otherwise appropriated for fiscal year 2025-2026, to support Amos House in
4 carrying out its mission to provide emergency food and shelter services, and to help people help
5 themselves out of oppression, homelessness, and poverty through vital services and results-
6 oriented programs; and the state controller is hereby authorized and directed to draw orders upon
7 the general treasurer for the payment of said sum, or so much thereof as maybe from time to time
8 required, upon receipt of properly authenticated vouchers.
