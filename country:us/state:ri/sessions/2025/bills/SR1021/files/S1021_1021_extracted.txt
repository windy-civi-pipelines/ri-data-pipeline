S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
S E N A T E R E S O L U T I O N
Introduced By: Senator Lammis J. Vargas
Date Introduced: May 05, 2025
Referred To: Placed on the Senate Consent Calendar
1 WHEREAS, Based in the City of Providence, the Rhode Island YMCA Alliance is a
2 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that is dedicated to supporting and promoting the mission of all
3 YMCAs in our State, from Westerly to Newport, and Kent County to Smithfield; and
4 WHEREAS, The organization works to strengthen YMCAs through collaboration and
5 advocacy in order to better serve our communities; and
6 WHEREAS, On Tuesday, April 29th of 2025, staff representing all the State’s YMCAs
7 will be celebrating “Y Day at the State House” with information on the wide variety of healthy
8 programming and education offered by the various branches, to help improve the lives of young
9 people, seniors, families, and individuals; and
10 WHEREAS, Rhode Island’s YMCAs have had a multifaceted positive impact on youth
11 development, healthy living, and social responsibility through after-school care, summer camps,
12 family events and academic enrichment and development; and
13 WHEREAS, Rhode Island's YMCAs have had a tangible impact on the lives of
14 generations of Rhode Islanders and stand as “a beacon of hope and opportunity” for the people of
15 our State; now, therefore be it
16 RESOLVED, That this Senate of the State of Rhode Island hereby celebrates “Y Day at
17 the State House” and thanks the staff and leadership of all our State’s YMCAs for their dedication
18 to “fostering the next generation of leaders, innovators, and changemakers” in our State; and be it
19 further
20 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized and directed to
1 transmit a duly certified copy of this resolution to Karen Santilli, YMCA Greater Providence
2 CEO.
