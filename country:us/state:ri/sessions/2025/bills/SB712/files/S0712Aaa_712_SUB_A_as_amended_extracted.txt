2025 -- S 0712 SUBSTITUTE A AS AMENDED
========
LC002323/SUB A
========
S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
NEW SHOREHAM
Introduced By: Senators DiMario, and Euer
Date Introduced: March 07, 2025
Referred To: Senate Housing & Municipal Government
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 46-15 of the General Laws entitled "Water Resources Management"
2 is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 46-15-24 New Shoreham water district obligations.
4 The commissioners of the New Shoreham Water district, governed by its board of water
5 commissioners, may specifically limit its public water system's obligation to supply water only to
6 those applicants who reside within its designated and described service area as opposed to the entire
7 land area of the municipality.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC002323/SUB A
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO WATERS AND NAVIGATION -- WATER RESOURCES MANAGEMENT --
NEW SHOREHAM
***
1 This act would provide that municipal water supply entities, including New Shoreham, be
2 required to provide water service to only its designated service areas.
3 This act would take effect upon passage.
