JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
EXTENDING CONDOLENCES
Date Introduced: June 17, 2025
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, The House of Representatives has learned with sorrow of the unfortunate
2 passing of:
3 Hunter Dean Sande Representatives Newberry and Marszalkowski
4 Mary J. Carani Speaker Shekarchi
5 John J. Fattorusso Speaker Shekarchi
6 Anthony P. Corsi Speaker Shekarchi
7 M. Dorothy Dansereau Speaker Shekarchi
8 Gerald R. Raposa Speaker Shekarchi
9 Paul A. Gingras Speaker Shekarchi
10 Pasquale T. DeFusco, Jr. Speaker Shekarchi
11 Julie M. Mancini Maley Speaker Shekarchi
12 Margaret M. D’Alfonso, Esq. Speaker Shekarchi
13 NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the
14 State of Rhode Island extends its sincerest condolences and expression of sympathy to the
15 bereaved; and be it further
16 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is respectfully requested to
17 transmit a properly inscribed memorial to the bereaved.
