LC002900

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
EXPIRATION DATES OF THE SPECIAL LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO STUDY
HOUSING AFFORDABILITY
Introduced By: Representatives Speakman, and Giraldo
Date Introduced: May 09, 2025
Referred To: House Municipal Government & Housing
1 RESOLVED, That the special legislative commission created by resolution No. 140 and
2 passed by the House of Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2021, and approved March 3
3 25, 2021, entitled "House Resolution Creating a Special Legislative Commission to Study the
4 Low and Moderate Income Housing Act" and as amended by resolution No. 066 and passed by
5 the House of Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2022, and approved January 25, 2022,
6 entitled "House Resolution Extending the Reporting and Expiration Dates for the Special
7 Legislative Commission to Study the Low and Moderate Income Housing Act" and as amended
8 by resolution No. 229 and passed by the House of Representatives at its January session, A.D.
9 2023, and approved April 6, 2023, entitled “House Resolution Extending the Reporting And
10 Expiration Dates and Amending the Purpose, Name, and Membership of the Special Legislative
11 Commission to Study The Low And Moderate Income Housing Act” is hereby authorized to
12 continue its study and make a report to the House of Representatives on or before June 5, 2027,
13 and said commission shall expire on June 26, 2027; and be it further
14 RESOLVED, That the time for reporting authorized by resolution No. 140 passed by the
15 House of Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2021, and approved March 25, 2021, and as
16 amended by resolution No. 066 passed by the House of Representatives at its January session,
17 A.D. 2022, and approved January 25, 2022, and as amended by resolution No. 229 passed by the
18 House of Representatives at its January session, A.D. 2023, and approved April 6, 2023, be and
19 the same is hereby rescinded; and be it further
1 RESOLVED, The composition of said house commission is hereby increased from
2 eighteen (18) members to twenty-one (21) members by adding: one member who shall be the
3 Rhode Island Secretary of Housing, or designee; one member who shall be the President and
4 CEO of United Way of Rhode Island, or designee; and one member who shall be the President
5 and CEO of the Rhode Island Public Expenditure Council, or designee.
========

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
AMENDING THE MEMBERSHIP AND EXTENDING THE REPORTING AND
EXPIRATION DATES OF THE SPECIAL LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO STUDY
HOUSING AFFORDABILITY
***
1 This resolution would increase the membership from eighteen (18) to twenty-one (21),
2 and extend the reporting and expiration dates of the Commission to Study Housing Affordability
3 from June 7, 2025, to June 5, 2027, and would expire on June 26, 2027.
