LC002228

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
S E N A T E R E S O L U T I O N
ISLAND DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION (RIDOT), AND THE RHODE ISLAND
DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION TO EXPLORE INCREASING THE
MASSACHUSETTS BAY TRANSPORTATION AUTHORITY’S (MBTA) EVENING
SERVICE TO THE WICKFORD JUNCTION/PROVIDENCE/STOUGHTON LINE ON
WEEKDAYS AND INITIATING A WEEKEND SCHEDULE
Introduced By: Senators Sosnowski, DiMario, Valverde, Euer, Kallman, and Pearson
Date Introduced: March 07, 2025
Referred To: Senate Housing & Municipal Government
1 WHEREAS, The Rhode Island Department of Transportation (RIDOT) coordinates with
2 transportation partner agencies such as RIPTA, Amtrak and the MBTA to promote driving
3 alternatives through public bus and rail service; and
4 WHEREAS, Currently, The MBTA Providence/Stoughton Line and RIPTA bus routes
5 are the main ways to travel between Providence and Wickford Junction, Rhode Island; and
6 WHEREAS, The MBTA’s Providence/Stoughton Line connects Providence, T.F. Green
7 Airport, and Wickford Junction to Boston and is the longest and busiest line on the MBTA
8 Commuter Rail system; and
9 WHEREAS, On weekdays, as of February 2022, the Providence/Stoughton Line runs 20
10 round trips between Boston and Providence, with approximately half of those trips continuing on
11 to Wickford Junction; and
12 WHEREAS, On weekends, the Providence/Stoughton Line runs nine round trips between
13 Boston and Providence, but does not serve Wickford Junction; and
14 WHEREAS, Taking the commuter rail avoids highway traffic, parking in downtown
15 Providence or Boston, and means less congestion on the busy Route 4 and I-95 highways and in
16 downtown Providence, and helps to minimize commuters' environmental footprint; now,
17 therefore be it
1 RESOLVED, That this Senate of the State of Rhode Island hereby requests the Governor,
2 the Director of RIDOT, and the General Manager of the MBTA to explore the potential for
3 increasing the Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority’s Wickford
4 Junction/Providence/Stoughton evening line on weekdays, initiating a weekend schedule, and to
5 report their findings to the Senate on or before June 1, 2025; and be it further
6 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is authorized ad directed to
7 transmit a duly certified copy of this resolution to the Governor of Rhode Island, the Director of
8 RIDOT, and the General Manager of the Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority.
