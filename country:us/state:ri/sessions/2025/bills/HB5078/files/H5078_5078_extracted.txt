LC000309
========
S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO HUMAN SERVICES -- MEDICAL ASSISTANCE
Noret, Read, Kennedy, and Hull
Date Introduced: January 16, 2025
Referred To: House Finance
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 40-8 of the General Laws entitled "Medical Assistance" is hereby
2 amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 40-8-33. Licensed independent clinical social workers- services covered..
4 Notwithstanding any general law, public law, special law, rule or regulation to the contrary,
5 Medicaid reimbursement shall be provided under this chapter for services by licensed independent
6 clinical social workers and such workers shall be entitled to enroll and bill Medicaid fee-for-service
7 directly for authorized and/or covered services provided to adults with Medicaid fee-for-service
8 coverage. The director is hereby directed to develop and promulgate a fee schedule and any
9 necessary rules or regulations required to implement the provisions of this section.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC000309
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO HUMAN SERVICES -- MEDICAL ASSISTANCE
***
1 This act would provide that licensed independent clinical social workers be able to enroll
2 and bill Medicaid directly for covered services provided to adults with Medicaid fee-for-service
3 coverage.
4 This act would take effect upon passage.
