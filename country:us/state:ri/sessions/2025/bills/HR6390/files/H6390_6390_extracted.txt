____________
H O U S E R E S O L U T I O N
EXTENDING CONDOLENCES
Date Introduced: June 05, 2025
Referred To: House read and passed
1 WHEREAS, The House of Representatives has learned with sorrow of the unfortunate
2 passing of:
3 Ronald A. Cardillo Representative Fellela
4 George E. “Perky” Mayhew, Jr. Representative Kennedy
5 Wayne Michael Morgan Representative Kennedy
6 Norman T. Brien Representative Lombardi
7 Amalia DiGiacinto Representative Costantino
8 Arthur L. Bentley Representative Fellela
9 NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, That this House of Representatives of the
10 State of Rhode Island extends its sincerest condolences and expression of sympathy to the
11 bereaved; and be it further
12 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of State be and hereby is respectfully requested to
13 transmit a properly inscribed memorial to the bereaved.
