S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
J O I N T R E S O L U T I O N
Introduced By: Representative Robert E. Craven
Date Introduced: February 12, 2025
Referred To: House Finance
1 WHEREAS, Nora’s Haven provides vital services to survivors of abuse and violence in
2 Rhode Island by providing essential resources to assist in navigating support systems; and
3 WHEREAS, Survivors often struggle with economic barriers, including limited job
4 opportunities, and inadequate financial assistance; and
5 WHEREAS, In addition, Nora’s Haven prioritizes outreach to those facing systematic
6 challenges including people of color, individuals with disabilities, limited English proficiency,
7 older adults, the LGBTQ+ communities, residents of low-income neighborhoods, and unhoused
8 individuals; and
9 WHEREAS, In order to meet the needs of those fleeing abusive situations as well as
10 myriad additional challenges, $500,000 is essential and would cover the following:
11 • Emergency Assistance including housing, transportation, and legal services;
12 • Basic needs including food, medicine, clothing, etc.;
13 • Other client support including training, therapy, and class supplies; and
14 • Administrative salaries, office rental, etc.; now, therefore be it
15 RESOLVED, That there is hereby appropriated, out of money in the treasury not
16 otherwise appropriated for the fiscal year 2025-2026, the sum of five hundred thousand dollars
17 ($500,000) to fund the vital services provided by Nora’s Haven; and the state controller is hereby
18 authorized and directed to draw their orders upon the general treasurer for the payment of said
1 sum, or so much thereof as may be from time to time required, upon receipt of properly
2 authenticated vouchers.
