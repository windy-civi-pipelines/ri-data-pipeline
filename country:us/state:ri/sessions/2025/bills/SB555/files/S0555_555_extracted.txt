2025 -- S 0555
========
LC001671
========
S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
Introduced By: Senators Sosnowski, Raptakis, Tikoian, LaMountain, Felag, and Burke
Date Introduced: February 26, 2025
Referred To: Senate Judiciary
(Judiciary)
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 31-27-4 of the General Laws in Chapter 31-27 entitled "Motor
2 Vehicle Offenses" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 31-27-4. Reckless driving and other offenses against public safety.
4 Any person who operates a motor vehicle recklessly so that the lives or safety of the public
5 might be endangered, or operates a vehicle in an attempt to elude or flee from a traffic officer or
6 police vehicle, shall be guilty of: a misdemeanor for the first conviction and a felony for the second
7 and each subsequent conviction.
8 (1) A misdemeanor for the first conviction; and
9 (2) A felony for the second and each subsequent conviction.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC001671
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO MOTOR AND OTHER VEHICLES -- MOTOR VEHICLE OFFENSES
***
1 This act would distinguish, in two (2) separate subsections, a first offense for reckless
2 driving, which is a misdemeanor, and a second or subsequent offense for reckless driving, which
3 is a felony.
4 This act would take effect upon passage.
