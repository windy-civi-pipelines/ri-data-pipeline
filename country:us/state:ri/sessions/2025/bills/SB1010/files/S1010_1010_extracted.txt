S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
ENTITLED "AN ACT TO INCORPORATE THE WESTERN COVENTRY FIRE DISTRICT"
Introduced By: Senators Rogers, Raptakis, E Morgan, de la Cruz, and Paolino
Date Introduced: May 02, 2025
Referred To: Senate Housing & Municipal Government
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. An Act Passed at the January Session A.D. 1942 entitled “An Act to
2 Incorporate the Western Coventry Fire District”, as from time to time amended, is hereby further
3 amended to read as follows:
4 Section 13. Vote to approve district merger, consolidation, or transition to municipal fire
5 department or consolidated fire district.
6 (a) Notwithstanding any other law, the District shall not be merged, joined, consolidated,
7 transitioned, or otherwise made a part of a municipal fire department of the Town of Coventry, or
8 consolidated with any other fire district without an all-day referendum and the affirmative vote of
9 the electors of the District in support thereof, said vote to be supervised by the Board of Directors.
10 (b) The all-day referendum shall be called in the same manner as Special Meetings of the
11 District as set forth in Section 6 hereof.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========

EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
IN AMENDMENT OF AN ACT PASSED AT THE JANUARY SESSION A.D. 1942
ENTITLED "AN ACT TO INCORPORATE THE WESTERN COVENTRY FIRE DISTRICT"
***
1 This act would amend the public law incorporating the Western Coventry Fire District to
2 require a referendum and affirmative vote of the fire district electors prior to any merge or
3 consolidation of the district with any other district or with the fire department of the town of
4 Coventry.
5 This act would take effect upon passage.
