S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
S E N A T E R E S O L U T I O N
LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO STUDY AND REVIEW RHODE ISLAND'S MINIMUM
WAGE
Introduced By: Senators Raptakis, Ciccone, Felag, Burke, McKenney, Pearson, Paolino,
Appollonio, Tikoian, and Sosnowski
Date Introduced: February 07, 2025
Referred To: Senate Labor & Gaming
1 RESOLVED, That the special legislative commission created by resolution No. 235
2 passed by the Senate at its January session, A.D. 2024, and approved April 30, 2024, entitled
3 "SENATE RESOLUTION CREATING A SPECIAL LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO
4 STUDY AND REVIEW RHODE ISLAND'S MINIMUM WAGE" is hereby authorized to
5 continue its study and make a report to the Senate on or before May 4, 2026, and said commission
6 shall expire on January 4, 2027; and be it further
7 RESOLVED, That the time for reporting authorized by resolution No. 235 passed by the
8 Senate at its January session, A.D. 2024, and approved April 30, 2024, be and the same is hereby
9 rescinded.
========
LC001129
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
S E N A T E R E S O L U T I O N
EXTENDING THE REPORTING AND EXPIRATION DATES OF THE SPECIAL
LEGISLATIVE COMMISSION TO STUDY AND REVIEW RHODE ISLAND'S MINIMUM
WAGE
***
1 This resolution would extend the reporting and expiration dates of the special legislative
2 commission to study Rhode Island’s minimum wage from May 4, 2025, to May 4, 2026, and said
3 commission would expire on January 4, 2027.
