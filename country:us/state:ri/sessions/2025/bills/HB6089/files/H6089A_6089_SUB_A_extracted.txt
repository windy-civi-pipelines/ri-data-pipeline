LC002380/SUB A

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
Introduced By: Representatives O'Brien, Dawson, Slater, Solomon, Casimiro, Kazarian,
Alzate, Stewart, Baginski, and Biah
Date Introduced: March 14, 2025
Referred To: House Judiciary
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 4-1-9 of the General Laws in Chapter 4-1 entitled "Cruelty to
2 Animals" is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 4-1-9. Animal fighting.
4 Any person who causes or encourages the fighting of any bird, dog, or animal with any
5 other bird, dog, or animal, or keeps or maintains any place for the fighting of birds, dogs, or animals,
6 or who knowingly permits, or suffers, any fight to be had on his or her premises or on premises
7 under his or her control, or makes any bet or lays any wager of any kind upon the result of that
8 fight, shall be fined not exceeding one thousand dollars ($1,000) or be imprisoned not exceeding
9 two (2) years, or both, for the first offense, and for a second and any subsequent offense shall be
10 fined not less than one thousand dollars ($1,000) nor more than five thousand dollars ($5,000) or
11 be imprisoned not exceeding two (2) five (5) years, or both. In addition, every person convicted of
12 a first offense shall be required to perform twenty (20) hours of community service. Every person
13 convicted of a subsequent offense shall be required to perform fifty (50) hours of community
14 service. The community service penalty shall not be suspended or deferred.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC002380/SUB A
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO ANIMALS AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY -- CRUELTY TO ANIMALS
***
1 This act would increase the imprisonment penalty for animal fighting from two (2) to five
2 (5) years for a subsequent conviction under § 4-1-9 and would also require mandatory community
3 service.
4 This act would take effect upon passage.
