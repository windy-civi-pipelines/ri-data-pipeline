LC002898

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
NAMING ALL NEW BUILDINGS, BRIDGES, EDIFICES AND OTHER STATE
CONSTRUCTIONS
Introduced By: Representatives Knight, and Speakman
Date Introduced: May 09, 2025
Referred To: House Special Legislation
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 22-7.4 of the General Laws entitled "Permanent Joint Committee on
2 Naming All New Buildings, Bridges, Edifices and Other State Constructions" is hereby amended
3 by adding thereto the following section:
4 22-7.4-157. Cpl. Richard C. Brule Bridge.
5 The East Bay Bike Path Bridge, located in the Town of Warren and which spans over the
6 Palmer River in the Town of Warren, shall hereafter be named the Cpl. Richard C. Brule Bridge.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC002898
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO GENERAL ASSEMBLY -- PERMANENT JOINT COMMITTEE ON
NAMING ALL NEW BUILDINGS, BRIDGES, EDIFICES AND OTHER STATE
CONSTRUCTIONS
***
1 This act would rename the East Bay Bike Path Bridge, located in the Town of Warren, as
2 the Cpl. Richard C. Brule Bridge.
3 This act would take effect upon passage.
