LC000038

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO SOLEMNIZATION OF MARRIAGES
Date Introduced: January 10, 2025
Referred To: House Special Legislation
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any other general or special law to the contrary, Ryan Kenny
2 and Brenna Sorkin may join Simran Bhajan Nebhnani and Louis Charles Miyara in marriage within
3 the City of Newport, Rhode Island, on or about April 26, 2025. Ryan Kenny and Brenna Sorkin are
4 hereby authorized and empowered to join the foregoing persons in marriage pursuant to and in
5 accordance with Chapter 3 of Title 15 of the General Laws, entitled "Solemnization of Marriages."
6 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC000038
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO SOLEMNIZATION OF MARRIAGES
***
1 This act would allow Ryan Kenny and Brenna Sorkin to join Simran Bhajan Nebhnani and
2 Louis Charles Miyara in marriage on April 26, 2025, within the City of Newport, Rhode Island.
3 This act would take effect upon passage.
