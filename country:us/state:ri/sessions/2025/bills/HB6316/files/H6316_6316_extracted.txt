2025 -- H 6316

LC002550

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
COMMISSION-NATURAL GAS SUPPLIERS
Introduced By: Representatives Chippendale, Paplauskas, Newberry, and Place
Date Introduced: May 09, 2025
Referred To: House Corporations
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 39-1 of the General Laws entitled "Public Utilities Commission" is
2 hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 39-1-2.2. Long-term contracts for natural gas.
4 The public utility known as “Rhode Island Energy” shall enter into at least one long-term
5 contract, for not less than four (4) years, for the procurement of natural gas pipeline capacity with
6 an interstate pipeline operator.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC002550
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO PUBLIC UTILITIES AND CARRIERS -- PUBLIC UTILITIES
COMMISSION-NATURAL GAS SUPPLIERS
***
1 This act would require Rhode Island Energy to enter into at least one long-term contract,
2 for at least a four (4) year period, to procure natural gas pipeline capacity with an interstate pipeline
3 operator.
4 This act would take effect upon passage.
