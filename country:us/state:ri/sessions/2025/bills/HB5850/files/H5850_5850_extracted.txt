Section 1:
2025 -- H 5850
========
LC001641
========
S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
SUBSTANCE USE DISORDERS
Introduced By: Representatives Shallcross Smith, Tanzi, Read, Kislak, Alzate, Slater,
Ajello, Stewart, Fogarty, and Hull
Date Introduced: February 28, 2025
Referred To: House Health & Human Services
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 27-38.2 of the General Laws entitled "Insurance Coverage for Mental
2 Illness and Substance Use Disorders" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 27-38.2-7. Reimbursement from private insurers.
4 Every individual or group health insurance contract, plan, or policy delivered, issued, or
5 renewed by an insurer, health maintenance organization, nonprofit or for-profit health service
6 corporation on or after January 1, 2026, that provides benefits to individual subscribers and
7 members within the state, or to all group members having a principal place of employment within
8 the state, shall reimburse child service providers for therapy services offered through EOHHS
9 certified Kids Connect/Therapeutic Services.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
LC001641
========
EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
A N A C T
RELATING TO INSURANCE -- INSURANCE COVERAGE FOR MENTAL ILLNESS AND
SUBSTANCE USE DISORDERS
***
1 This act would require every individual or group health insurance plan on or after January
2 1, 2026, that provides benefits to reimburse child service providers for therapy services offered
3 through EOHHS certified Kids Connect/Therapeutic Services.
4 This act would take effect upon passage.
