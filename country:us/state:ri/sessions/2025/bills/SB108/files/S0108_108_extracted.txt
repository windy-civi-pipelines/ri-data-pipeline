LC000142

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
PROJECTS
Introduced By: Senators Ujifusa, Tikoian, Lawson, Euer, Pearson, Valverde, E Morgan,
Ciccone, Felag, and Mack
Date Introduced: January 31, 2025
Referred To: Senate Finance
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 2-10 of the General Laws entitled "State Forests and Forestry
2 Projects" is hereby amended by adding thereto the following section:
3 2-10-14. Expansion of department of environmental management personnel.
4 The general assembly shall appropriate, in the fiscal year 2026 budget, a sum sufficient to
5 fund ten (10) additional full-time equivalent ("FTE") positions, to be assigned to the department of
6 environmental management (the "department") as well as fund their necessary equipment and
7 supplies. It is the intent of the general assembly that these positions be used in part to focus, support,
8 and expand on the department's efforts in the areas of woodlands, forests, and forestry projects.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
