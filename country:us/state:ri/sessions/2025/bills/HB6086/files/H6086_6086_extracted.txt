S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
BARRENGOS FOUNDATION
Introduced By: Representatives Cruz, Alzate, and Stewart
Date Introduced: March 14, 2025
Referred To: House Corporations
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. The time within which The Kane-Barrengos Foundation, incorporated on
2 July 16, 1969, shall file the reports for the years 1998, 1999, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2003, 2004, 2005,
3 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021,
4 2022, 2023 ,2024, and 2025 as required under the provisions of Chapter 6 of Title 7 of the General
5 Laws, 1956, is hereby extended to the 31st day of December, A.D., 2025, if said corporation shall
6 file in the office of the secretary of state before December 31, 2025, the reports which it should
7 have filed in the said years and shall pay to the secretary of state the filing fees therefor, having
8 previously thereto paid to the general treasurer for the use of the state such sum as the attorney
9 general shall decide should be paid by said corporation as a compromise for the penalty or penalties
10 for its failure to file such reports then the forfeiture or revocation of the charter of said corporation
11 shall thereupon, to wit, upon the filing of said reports and the payment of said fees, be vacated and
12 said corporation shall thereupon be entitled to all of the privileges, benefits and powers and subject
13 to the same duties and liabilities as though no forfeiture or revocation of its said charter had taken
14 place.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
