LC002982

S T A T E O F R H O D E I S L A N D
IN GENERAL ASSEMBLY
JANUARY SESSION, A.D. 2025
____________
A N A C T
RELATING TO SOLEMNIZATION OF MARRIAGES
Introduced By: Senator Jessica de la Cruz
Date Introduced: June 03, 2025
It is enacted by the General Assembly as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any other general or special law to the contrary, the
2 Honorable Thomas J. Paolino may join Mariah Marie Paolino and Conner Joseph Gerlach in
3 marriage within the Town of Lincoln, Rhode Island on or about June 13, 2025. The Honorable
4 Thomas J. Paolino is hereby authorized and empowered to join the foregoing persons in marriage
5 pursuant to and in accordance with Chapter 3 of Title 15 of the General Laws, entitled
6 “Solemnization of Marriages.”
7 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon passage.
========
